Beer Spotlight

This page is to highlight two of our beers and two beers of craft breweries across the country that we are especially enjoying right now!

Beers we Brew

Hide-Behind Pale Ale: Inspired by Bell's Two-Hearted Pale Ale and designed by Cameron, our VP of Education, this pale ale is light but packs a load of flavor. We have aged the pale on grapefruit to give it something extra, and it reminded us of Ballast Point's Grapefruit Sculpin. The alcohol content is around 6% and it's by far the best beer we have brewed so far.

Pamola Porter: Created for the Super Bowl, this beer has a low alcohol content of 4%, drinks like Bud Light, but has the flavor of a porter you would expect to get from a pub in London. It has a roasty flavor with notes of butter pecan.

Beers we Drink

Hi-Wire 10W-40: As the name suggests, it's thick like motor oil. An imperial stout brewed with chocolate and vanilla, this beer is a favorite of all Snolligoster Brew Club members. Hi-Wire Brewing Company is located in Asheville, NC.

21st Amendment Marooned on Hog Island: An oyster stout filtered over oyster shells from Hog Island in California, this beer reminds a few of us of those great days drinking oyster stouts at beer festivals. 21st Amendment Brewing Company is located in San Fransisco, CA.
